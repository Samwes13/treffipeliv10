Treffipeli — Privacy Policy (English)
Last updated: 2025-12-05

1. Data we collect
- Anonymous account ID for gameplay.
- Optional username you provide.
- Purchase and subscription status from the store via RevenueCat.
- Device identifiers needed for ads/analytics (from store SDKs such as Google Mobile Ads and RevenueCat).

2. How we use data
- To run the game, restore sessions, and verify purchases.
- To show ads (if applicable) and measure performance.

3. Sharing
- RevenueCat receives purchase-related data to manage entitlements.
- App Store or Google Play handle billing data.
- Ad/analytics partners (e.g., Google Mobile Ads) may receive device identifiers to serve and measure ads.
We do not sell personal data.

4. Storage and retention
Data is stored by our service providers and kept only as needed for the purposes above or legal requirements.

5. Your choices
- You can delete the app to stop data collection on that device.
- You may request removal of in-app data where applicable by contacting support.
- You can limit ad tracking via your device settings.

6. Children
The app is not directed to children under 13. Do not use if you are under the minimum age in your region.

7. Contact
Support: info@treffipeli.fi
